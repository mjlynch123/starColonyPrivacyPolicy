1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information automatically when you use the app, including:

We may show you in-app messages, offers, or announcements about Star Colony features, updates, or related promotions. You can opt out of optional marketing messages at any time using the settings or instructions provided in those communications.

Star Colony is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
